### Q: What is the easiest way to travel between Flåm/Aurland and Balestrand?

A: You have two main options:
By car: It's about a 3-hour drive one way. Ferry times must be added (Hella-Dragsvik). The \[car ferry is an experience in itself] [(LINK TO FERRY INFO TABELL HERE, NOT VINTERTIME OR SUMMERTIME DATE)](https://www.skyss.no/globalassets/reise/rutetabellar/bat-og-ferje/b%C3%A5t-og-ferje/1046.pdf)! Be aware of roadworks and \[book a rental car now in good time] [(LINK TO CARENTAL IN FLAM/AURLAND HERE).](https://explore-flam.com/)
By express ferry (Norled): This is the easiest. The boat runs Bergen -> Balestrand -> Flåm. Guests can arrive in Balestrand at 11:50 AM, have lunch, fish at 3:00 PM, and return to Flåm the next day.

### Q: Which cultural sights can I see on the trip?

A: The area is full of cultural treasures. Everything in Balestrand (\[Kviknes Hotel](LINK TIL KVIKNES HOTEL), \[St. Olaf’s Church][(LINK THINGS TO DO IN BALESTRAND)](https://en.sognefjord.no/fjord-villages/balestrand/), meeting point) is within a 5-minute walk.
On the drive: Drive from Flåm to Vik to visit Hopperstad Stave Church [(THINGS TOR DO IN LAERDAL).](https://en.sognefjord.no/fjord-villages/laerdal/activities-attractions/) Take the ferry to Balestrand and see the giant statue of Fridtjov the Bold at the Vangsnes ferry terminal.
Alternative route: Consider the route via Borgund Stave Church in Lærdal or Urnes Stave Church if you are going north.

### Q: Why is an overnight stay recommended?

A: Flåm is great, but Balestrand is historical and relaxed. An overnight stay allows you to experience both places as "pearls on a string" and gives you the peace to enjoy both \[breakfast at Kviknes Hotel][(LINK TIL KVIKNES HOTEL RESTAURANT)](https://www.kviknes.no/restaurant-kviknes) and an evening stroll.
