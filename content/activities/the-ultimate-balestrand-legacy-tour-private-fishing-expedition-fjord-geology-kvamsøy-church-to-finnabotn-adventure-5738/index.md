"The day starts at 09:00 AM with our 'Balestrand Legacy Tour: Sognefjord Fishing Thrill – Family Bonding' program."

Lunch at Kviknes Hotell (ekstra)

After lunch14:00,  we board the "Miss Fjords." Our voyage commences as we set a course for the small, idyllic island of Kvamsøy

### Afternoon | Time Travel to Kvamsøy Church (c. 13th Century)

We dock at Kvamsøy and explore the charming Kvamsøy Church, a mediaeval stone church dating back to the 1200s. This is one of the smallest and oldest stone churches in the region, a hidden gem that tells stories from a bygone era.

### Fjord Geology & Titanic Forces

As we cross the fjord again, we pass dramatic cliff formations. Captain Tor recounts the incredible geological forces that shaped this landscape. We sail through an area rich with evidence of the Caledonian plate collision from millions of years ago – forces that created mountain ranges once higher than today’s Himalayas!

### Deep Water & Local Heritage

We pass over one of the fjord's deepest sections, a staggering 4,000 feet (1,200 meters) straight down, en route to Finden. This area is known for more than just depth; it is linked to the story of Synnøve Finden, the enterprising woman who gave her name to one of Norway's most famous cheese brands.
Into the Magical Finnabotn

The journey continues through the exciting tidal current that leads into the fantastic and magical Finnabotn. This is hyper-nature at its most intense – a narrow fjord arm surrounded by steep, mighty mountains reflected in the crystal-clear, pure water. The silence here is almost magical.

### Activity: Slow Trolling in the Stillness

As we glide slowly into Finnabotn, we deploy the trolling lines. We fish here for trout, mackerel, and pollock. Surrounded by this intense environment, the fishing is as much about the peace and presence as the catch itself.

**Return: The Fjord's Muscles & the Fjord Horse's Spirit**

After stories in the inner fjord, we cross the Sognefjord back towards Balestrand. Perhaps we feel the afternoon sea breeze build – a reminder of the fjord's own "muscles" and forces, with sea spray that refreshes the senses.

Our boat, "Miss Fjords," is built for exactly this. It is like the Norwegian Fjord Horse itself – Norway's national symbol that built the fjord landscape: strong, safe, great with passengers of all ages, and stylish on the fjord. It handles the elements with a calm resilience.
